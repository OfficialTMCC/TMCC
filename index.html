We are the Townview Magnet Coding Club

Join our adventure of code and come learn to code with us

The Townview Magnet Coding Club is a Townview-wide club where anyone can learn how to code.
The TMCC which it is better known was started by 2 people who wanted to unite the 6 Townview schools through the way of software development
In the TMCC you will learn all sorts of coding languages from html to javascript and even python.

The TMCC is held on Tuesdays after school from 4:30 to 5:20 hope to see you there!
